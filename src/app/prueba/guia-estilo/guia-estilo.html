Input Fields

Default Input

We'll never share your email with anyone else.
<div class="input-group"> <label class="input-label">Email Address</label> <input type="email" class="input-field" placeholder="Enter your email"> <span class="input-helper">We'll never share your email.</span> </div>

Error State

Username must be at least 3 characters long.
<div class="input-group"> <label class="input-label">Username</label> <input type="text" class="input-field input-error" value="ab"> <span class="input-error-text">Username must be at least 3 characters.</span> </div>

Success State

Password strength: Strong
<div class="input-group"> <label class="input-label">Password</label> <input type="password" class="input-field input-success" value="***"> <span class="input-success-text">Password strength: Strong</span> </div>

Textarea

Maximum 500 characters.
<div class="input-group"> <label class="input-label">Description</label> <textarea class="input-field" rows="4"></textarea> <span class="input-helper">Maximum 500 characters.</span> </div>

Cards

Basic Card

Card Title

This is the card body content. You can add any content here including text, images, or other components.

<div class="card"> <div class="card-header"> <h3 class="card-title">Card Title</h3> </div> <div class="card-body"> <p>Card body content...</p> </div> <div class="card-footer"> <button class="btn btn-primary btn-sm">Action</button> </div> </div>

Hoverable Card

Hover Me

This card has a hover effect. Try hovering over it to see the shadow and border change.

<div class="card card-hover"> <div class="card-header"> <h3 class="card-title">Hover Me</h3> </div> <div class="card-body"> <p>This card has a hover effect.</p> </div> </div>

Simple Card

Simple Card

A card doesn't need a header or footer. You can use just the body section for simpler layouts.

<div class="card"> <div class="card-body"> <h3 class="card-title mb-2">Simple Card</h3> <p>Card content...</p> </div> </div>
